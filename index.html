Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide school supplies-which we consider as essentials in the US-to underprivileged children in shelters in Pakistan. I decided to start this project to give back to my community in Pakistan. Growing up visiting my parents home country, l've seen necessities taken for granted in the US be considered luxuries in Pakistan. I believe that education should be a right for everyone, and access to school supplies is essential to enable learning. That's why we raise money to fund necessary school supplies for this shelter to create opportunities and promote their learning - Meshu Malik
